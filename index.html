About Me

Hi, I'm Hitesh Diwan, a passionate and detail-oriented B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering student at Sardar Patel University, Balaghat. I specialize in web development with a strong focus on React.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, PHP & MySql. I enjoy creating user-friendly, responsive, and scalable applications, and I'm skilled at integrating APIs, working with databases like MongoDB, and building full-stack projects. From multilingual news platforms to smart attendance systems, I love turning ideas into functional, polished products.

Beyond coding, I value problem-solving, clean design, and continuous learning—whether it's exploring new frameworks, experimenting with UI/UX, or optimizing backend performance. My goal is to craft digital experiences that are both innovative and impactful.
